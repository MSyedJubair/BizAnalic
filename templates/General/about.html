About BankAnalytics

BankAnalytics is a web platform that helps users understand their finances by uploading bank statements and visualizing spending, income, and savings trends in an interactive dashboard.

Easy Upload

Users can quickly upload bank statements in CSV, Excel, or PDF formats to begin their financial analysis immediately.

Detailed Analytics

Get deep insights into income, expenses, and savings trends with automatic categorization of transactions.

Interactive Charts

Visualize your finances through pie, bar, and line charts that highlight trends, spending categories, and savings patterns.

Export Reports

Download your analytics as PDF or Excel reports for personal tracking or sharing purposes.
